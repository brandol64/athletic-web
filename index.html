J.J. Watt

J.J. Watt is a former NFL player known for his incredible defensive skills. He played most of his career with the Houston Texans and is considered one of the greatest defensive ends in NFL history.

Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Justin James Watt
  • Born: March 22, 1989
  • Position: Defensive End
  • Teams: Houston Texans, Arizona Cardinals
  • Defensive Player of the Year: 3 Times
